DMCA Policy

Intellectual property protection.

Last Updated: April 2025

Aron B. Waters respects intellectual property rights and expects all users and visitors to do the same. This policy outlines how we handle claims of copyright infringement in accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and equivalent European legislation.

Our Content is Protected

All original content created and published by Aron B. Waters — including but not limited to coaching frameworks, written works, spoken word pieces, music, course materials, audio recordings, videos, workbooks, brand assets, and website content — is protected by copyright law.

Unauthorised reproduction, distribution, or commercial use of any of our content is strictly prohibited and will be pursued legally.

Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe that your copyrighted work has been used on our website or within our content in a way that constitutes copyright infringement, please submit a formal DMCA takedown notice to us with the following information:

  1. Your physical or electronic signature (or that of an authorised agent)
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed
  3. Identification of the specific material on our site that allegedly infringes your work, including its URL or location
  4. Your contact information: full name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address
  5. A statement that you have a good faith belief that the disputed use is not authorised by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law
  6. A statement, made under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorised to act on their behalf

Please note: submitting a false DMCA notice is a serious legal matter and may result in liability for damages.

How to Submit a Notice

Send your DMCA takedown notice by email to:

Email: legal@aronbwaters.com
Subject Line: DMCA Takedown Notice

Postal Address:
Aron B. Waters
Attn: Legal / DMCA
The Netherlands

Email is the fastest method. We aim to respond to all valid notices within 10 business days.

Counter Notices

If you believe your content was removed in error or misidentification, you may submit a counter notice. A valid counter notice must include:

  • Your physical or electronic signature
  • Identification of the material that was removed and its previous location
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed by mistake or misidentification
  • Your full name, address, telephone number, and email address
  • A statement consenting to jurisdiction of the relevant court in your area

Upon receiving a valid counter notice, we will notify the original complainant and, unless they pursue legal action within 14 business days, restore the removed content.

Repeat Infringers

We have a zero-tolerance policy toward repeat copyright infringers. Users who repeatedly violate intellectual property rights will have their access to all Aron B. Waters platforms and services permanently terminated.

Accurate Information

The DMCA requires that all notices be accurate and submitted in good faith. Submitting a false or misleading notice is a serious offence and may expose you to significant legal liability, including damages. We strongly encourage consulting with a legal professional before submitting any copyright claim.
